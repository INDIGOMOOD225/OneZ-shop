Welcome to the L@IR

The lair is fan page created solely by the Teo Burcl.
Purpose of this page is to show modern society the beauty behind old school games, hidden formulas behind the titles. The fascinating concept of perma-death and use of procedurally-generated content. Showcase of the Rogue-like genre that defined its legacy thus many modern devs are still inclined to keep the tradition by creating nostalgic games not only by visuals, but also by keeping the formula intact, punishing and yet rewarding at the same time. As it was meant to be. The Rogue-like concept can be found in different genres and can serve similar purpose.
